Defra provides £20,000 grants for installing a wind turbine on your property. To qualify, you must own a business, and that business must own the land where the turbine will be installed.

Use this service to:

  • check your eligibility to have a wind turbine
  • to apply for a grant

The application takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You will need:

  • your contact details.
  • the details about the property such as the size of it.

You can’t apply for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.

You can also apply by post.
